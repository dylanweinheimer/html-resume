Hobbies

Tabletop Roleplaying

From Dungeons & Dragons to Cyberpunk Red, if I get to create thrilling and hilarious stories with my friends
I am in. I started the hobby several years ago now and while I do not get to actively participate in it often, I
love every second I am playing, and I have actively sought out new roleplaying games over the years, with a
personal collection of 10+ unique games and 30+ prewritten adventures.

Board Games

While this hobby has a lot in common with tabletop roleplaying it is a far more attractive option to the average
person, so I often find it easier to participate in this, which has led to an even more extensive (and expensive)
collection forming over the years.

Retro Game Repair

This hobby is relatively new, I just started within the last year and have little experience with it so far, but the
experience I do have has been eye opening. I started with taking my childhood gameboy and seeing if I could get
it up and running again, after disassembling it, cleaning all the parts and replacing a few minor components I
was able to turn it on and get it to read games again. Which launched me head first into a new hobby I never
expected, repairing game cartridges and other devices, with the eventual goal being to completely modify these
old devices to have rechargeable batteries, LED screens, bluetooth audio, and more.
